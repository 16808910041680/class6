About

Hi! This is an autobiography of Sam, the fictional Codingal character.

Sam is a fictional character designed by Codingal to create absurdly weird stories on why we need to do our project for him. This has multiple flaws when presented with a child with personal opinions, but we'll get into that later. Anyways, our friend Sam here always asks the Codingal project completers to do their project for him, no matter how many favors we have given him. Eventually, I believe someone should tell him that coding services cost actual cash in this real world, and that Codingal should make more believable fictional characters, perhaps persuaded by this unimportant piece of post-formal text, but perhaps Codingal would also be forced to read this at some point, or at least one of it's staff.
